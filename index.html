Why

Reforestation is a big deal. In the past 100 years, we've lost over 50% of our forests. That's a whole lot of trees. And we need them back fast.

Reforestation is the process of planting and growing new trees in areas where they haven't grown before. It's also sometimes called afforestation, when you're planting trees in an area that used to have trees but doesn't anymore. Reforestation can help us reverse our planet's warming effects and bring back animal habitats that are disappearing at an alarming rate.

How

  • Plant a tree at your home or office
  • Set up a reforestation project at school or work
  • Start a fundraiser for reforestation efforts

Reforesting is important because it helps plants and animals live longer, healthier lives.
Reforesting also helps us by reducing carbon emissions into our atmosphere!
